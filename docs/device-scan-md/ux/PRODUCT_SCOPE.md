# Product Scope

## Purpose

Keep LineWatt Library focused as a professional engineering library for renewable energy equipment.

LineWatt Library is not a generic document platform, project workspace or AI assistant.

## Core Promise

> Create, manage, search, compare and distribute trusted Engineering Records.

## Library Responsibilities

- Upload datasheets.
- Compile Engineering Records.
- Review Engineering Records.
- Search Engineering Records.
- Compare two Engineering Records.
- Publish trusted central Engineering Records.
- Export Engineering Records and comparison outputs according to permissions.

## Product Areas

- Home: premium discovery and entry point.
- Engineering Search: search experience.
- Library: consume, read, discover, compare and export Engineering Records.
- My Library: subscriber private workspace.
- Central Engineering Workspace: platform engineering operations.
- Partner Portal: OEM/manufacturer operations and benchmarking.

## Explicitly Out Of Scope

- Engineering Data Rooms.
- Project Collaboration.
- Tender Management.
- Procurement.
- AI Assistant.
- Document Management.
- Project Workspaces.
- Engineering Calculations.
- Multi-document reasoning.

These belong to future LineWatt products, not LineWatt Library v1.

## Future Vision Note

Future LineWatt products may include:

- MCP Server.
- Engineering Services.
- Data Rooms.
- AI Engineering Assistant.

No implementation details are defined in this UX milestone.

## Permissions

- Guests can discover only.
- Subscribers can manage private records and compare visible records.
- Platform staff can operate Central Engineering Workspace.
- Partners can submit products and use published Central Library records for benchmarking.

## Edge Cases

- Comparison is read-only and limited to two products in v1.
- Partner benchmarking must not influence Engineering Search ranking.
- Ads and promotions must not affect validation, ranking or engineering recommendations.
- Private tenant records must not appear in public Library views.
