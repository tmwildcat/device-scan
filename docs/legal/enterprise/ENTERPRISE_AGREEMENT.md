## 1. Purpose and scope

This Agreement governs an Enterprise Customer's ordered Services and authorised users. Each Order identifies scope, term, Fees, seats, Workspaces, support, integrations, and any statement of work.

## 2. Applicable definitions and order of precedence

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md). A negotiated amendment, Order, this Agreement, DPA, SLA, and incorporated policies follow the [Precedence Matrix](../appendices/DOCUMENT_PRECEDENCE_MATRIX.md), with subject-specific DPA and SLA priority.

## 3. Access and administration

The Customer controls authorised users and must maintain accurate administrators, allocate least privilege, protect credentials, and promptly remove access. Use remains subject to the [Acceptable Use Policy](../user/ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md) and purchased limits.

## 4. Customer data and confidentiality

The Customer retains its rights in Customer User Content and grants the limited operational licence in the Terms. Private Datasets remain access-controlled. Each party protects Confidential Information using reasonable care and uses it only for the relationship, subject to lawful disclosure with notice where permitted.

## 5. Privacy, security, and support

The [Data Processing Addendum](../privacy/DATA_PROCESSING_ADDENDUM.md) applies only where executed and LineWatt acts as Processor. Security commitments are those verified in the Order and Data Processing Addendum schedule. Support and service levels apply only if specified in an Order and the [Service Level Agreement](./SERVICE_LEVEL_AGREEMENT.md).

## 6. API, MCP, and service changes

Integration access requires express enablement and the [API and MCP Terms](./API_AND_MCP_TERMS.md). LineWatt may evolve Services and deprecate interfaces with the notice stated in the Order; no public API or publicly exposed MCP entitlement exists by default in v1.

## 7. Fees, taxes, and audit

Fees, invoicing, taxes, payment timing, expenses, and renewal are in the Order. LineWatt may audit usage records proportionately to verify seats, scopes, and limits, protecting Customer confidentiality and operational security.

## 8. Term, suspension, and termination

Term and renewal follow each Order. Either party may terminate for uncured material breach under `[CURE PERIOD]` or insolvency where lawful. LineWatt may suspend proportionately for security risk, unlawful use, or overdue undisputed Fees after required notice. Exit export, assistance, return, and deletion are `[TO BE SPECIFIED IN ORDER/DPA]`.

Service warranty, disclaimer, IP/customer-data indemnities, claim control, liability caps and exclusions, insurance, and remedies are `[TO BE NEGOTIATED AND APPROVED]`. They must align with the Engineering Disclaimer, SLA, mandatory law, and the parties' risk allocation.

Each party complies with applicable law. Export-control, sanctions, anti-bribery, assignment, publicity, notices, force majeure, governing law, courts, and dispute terms are `[TO BE CONFIRMED]`. No purchase order boilerplate overrides an executed agreement unless accepted in writing.
