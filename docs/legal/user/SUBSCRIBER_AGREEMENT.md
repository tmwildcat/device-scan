3.3 LineWatt may evolve features. A materially adverse reduction during a prepaid term is subject to applicable law, Order commitments, and an appropriate remedy `[TO BE CONFIRMED]`.

## 4. My Private Datasets

4.1 A Subscriber may upload an authorised PDF, run DeviceScan, review a resulting Engineering Record, and store it in a Private Workspace within plan entitlements.

4.2 Private Datasets remain private by default. Upload, compilation, processing, or internal indexing does not publish them. Publication requires a separate deliberate submission, rights confirmation, and LineWatt review.

4.3 Storage, compilation, comparison, PAN/OND/JSON/CSV or other exports depend on availability and plan terms. Mixed-rights and Source Document restrictions apply to outputs.
