## 1. Purpose and scope

This Policy protects Users, engineering data, and Platform integrity and applies to every use of the Services.

## 2. Applicable definitions

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md).

## 3. Prohibited conduct

A User must not:

- use the Platform unlawfully, fraudulently, deceptively, or to infringe rights;
- upload Content without sufficient rights or knowingly falsify source, revision, review, or publication information;
- introduce malware, disrupt availability, probe beyond authorised testing, or bypass access, quota, entitlement, rate, or security controls;
- share credentials, impersonate another person or Manufacturer, or attempt to access another User's Private Dataset;
- scrape, bulk-harvest, cache, resell, or redistribute Content outside an express licence, Order, robots instruction, or API term;
- reverse engineer except to the extent a restriction is prohibited by law;
- misuse an API, MCP tool, automated agent, or export to create excessive load, evade scopes, or expose tenant data;
- use outputs as the sole basis for safety-critical design or action without independent verification; or
- interfere with review, validation, ranking, provenance, audit logs, or safety and recall notices.

## 4. Enforcement

LineWatt may investigate, preserve evidence, restrict Content, rate-limit, suspend, or terminate proportionately. Where lawful and safe, LineWatt will notify the affected User and offer a cure or appeal route. Illegal Content may be reported or disclosed as required by law.

## 5. Lawful rights and reporting

This Policy does not restrict non-waivable rights, legitimate interoperability, security research authorised by the [Responsible Disclosure Policy](../governance/RESPONSIBLE_DISCLOSURE_POLICY.md), or protected reporting. Report abuse to `[ABUSE CONTACT EMAIL]`.
