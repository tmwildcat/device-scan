## 1. Purpose and scope

This Agreement supplements the [Terms of Use](./TERMS_OF_USE.md) for a Registered User. It does not create a paid entitlement.

## 2. Applicable definitions

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md).

## 3. Registered access

3.1 A Registered User may use Public Library and Account features made available in the current free service description. Limits may apply to searches, saved items, uploads, compilation, Private Datasets, storage, comparisons, and exports.

3.2 Free features may change or end on reasonable notice where practicable. A Registered User has no entitlement to Subscriber, Enterprise, Manufacturer, API, or MCP features.

3.3 If upload or compilation is enabled, User Content licensing and privacy follow the Terms and Privacy Policy. Private Datasets remain private and cannot be published through ordinary free-account use.

## 4. Responsibilities and restrictions

The Registered User must secure the Account, use only personal authorised access, comply with the [Acceptable Use Policy](./ACCEPTABLE_USE_POLICY.md), and independently verify Engineering Data under the [Engineering Disclaimer](./ENGINEERING_DISCLAIMER.md).

## 5. Conversion, suspension, and closure

An accepted checkout may convert the User to a Subscriber under the [Subscriber Agreement](./SUBSCRIBER_AGREEMENT.md). LineWatt may suspend or close the Account under the Terms. Before closure, the User should export permitted data; post-closure access and deletion follow the retention policy and stated product controls.
