## 1. Purpose and scope

These definitions apply throughout the LineWatt Library legal framework unless a document expressly states otherwise. They do not expand a service, entitlement, or legal right.

## 2. Parties and users

- **Authorised User** means an individual whom an Enterprise Customer, Manufacturer, Publisher, or other account owner authorises to use specified Services on its behalf.
- **Enterprise Customer** means an organisation purchasing Services under an Enterprise Agreement and Order.
- **LineWatt** means [LINEWATT LEGAL ENTITY].
- **Manufacturer** means an equipment manufacturer admitted to the controlled manufacturer workflow.
- **Publisher** means a Manufacturer or other authorised organisation permitted to submit content for review.
- **Registered User** means a User holding an Account without an active paid plan.
- **Subscriber** means a User holding an active paid Subscription.
- **User** means a Visitor, Registered User, Subscriber, Manufacturer representative, Publisher representative, Enterprise Customer authorised user, or other person accessing the Platform.
- **Verified Manufacturer** means a Manufacturer whose identity or authority LineWatt has verified under the Verified Manufacturer Policy; it is not a product endorsement.
- **Visitor** means a person using publicly accessible Platform features without an Account.

## 3. Platform and services

- **DeviceScan** means LineWatt's compiler workflow that uses automated, rules-based, AI-assisted, and potentially human-reviewed processing to derive structured data from a Source Document.
- **Documentation** means user, technical, integration, and operational documentation that LineWatt makes available for the Services.
- **Output** means an Engineering Record, Compiled Datasheet, export, comparison, response, or other result generated or delivered through the Services.
- **Platform** means LineWatt Library, DeviceScan, and associated websites, applications, authorised APIs, MCP tools, and administrative workflows made available by LineWatt.
- **Public Engineering Record** means an Engineering Record deliberately published in the Public Library through an authorised workflow.
- **Public Library** means the publicly or broadly accessible collection of published Engineering Records, metadata, links, and authorised Content within the Platform.
- **Services** means the Platform features LineWatt makes available under an applicable agreement, plan description, or Order.

## 4. Engineering information

- **Canonical Dataset** means Engineering Data organised, normalised, selected, or arranged using LineWatt schemas, taxonomies, validation rules, and data models. The term does not determine ownership of individual facts or Third-Party Content.
- **Compiled Datasheet** means a LineWatt-generated structured Output produced from a Source Document and related inputs. It is not the original Source Document even where it incorporates or reflects information from that Source Document, and it may contain mixed rights.
- **Engineering Data** means technical facts, values, units, product identifiers, characteristics, and derived or normalised technical information.
- **Engineering Metadata** means provenance, classification, status, confidence, review, validation, revision, and other contextual information associated with Engineering Data.
- **Engineering Record** means a structured Platform record containing Engineering Data and Engineering Metadata associated with equipment or a Source Document.
- **Official Source Document** means a Source Document issued, authorised, or verified as official by the relevant Manufacturer or rights holder.
- **Source Document** means a document used as an input or reference source, whether or not it has been verified as official, current, complete, accurate, or authoritative, such as a datasheet, manual, certificate, drawing, or revision notice.

## 5. Content and intellectual property

- **Content** means text, documents, data, images, marks, feedback, and other material submitted to, processed by, or displayed through the Platform.
- **LineWatt Materials** means Platform software, schemas, data models, taxonomies, dictionaries, validation rules, review annotations, confidence methods, database selection and arrangement, search indexes, documentation, branding, and original presentation controlled by LineWatt.
- **Manufacturer Content** means Content supplied or authorised by a Manufacturer, including Source Documents, marks, product images, and product information.
- **Third-Party Content** means Content owned or controlled by a person other than LineWatt or the User submitting it.
- **User Content** means Content a User submits or makes available, excluding LineWatt Materials.

## 6. Accounts, plans, and workspaces

- **Account** means the credentialed profile through which a User accesses non-public Services.
- **Credentials** means passwords, tokens, keys, secrets, certificates, or other authentication information used to access an Account, API, MCP tool, or Service.
- **Order** means an ordering document, online checkout record, or statement of work specifying purchased Services.
- **Order Form** means a document executed by LineWatt and an Enterprise Customer that identifies ordered Services and commercial terms; it is a form of Order.
- **Plan** means a published or ordered package of Service entitlements, limits, and commercial terms.
- **Private Dataset** means a non-public dataset or Engineering Record stored in a Private Workspace, including a user-compiled dataset; processing or internal indexing does not make it public.
- **Private Workspace** means an access-controlled Workspace in which Authorised Users manage Private Datasets and related Source Documents. Its interface may be presented as **My Private Datasets**.
- **Subscription** means a paid, time-limited entitlement to specified Services under a plan or Order.
- **Subscription Term** means the period during which a Subscription remains active under the applicable Plan or Order.
- **Workspace** means an access-controlled Platform context assigned to one or more Users, including a Private Workspace or organisation workspace.

## 7. Processing and privacy

- **AI-Assisted Processing** means use of machine-learning or generative-model technology as one component of classification, extraction, normalisation, validation, search, support, or related processing.
- **Data Protection Law** means all Applicable Law governing the Processing of Personal Data, including GDPR and related legislation where applicable.
- **GDPR** means Regulation (EU) 2016/679, together with any applicable UK implementation or successor legislation where relevant.
- **Processing** has the meaning assigned by applicable Data Protection Law.
- **Controller**, **Data Subject**, **Personal Data**, **Personal Data Breach**, **Processor**, and **Subprocessor** have the meanings assigned by applicable Data Protection Law.

## 8. APIs, MCP, and integrations

- **API** means an application programming interface authorised by LineWatt, whether internal, first-party, or later offered under an Order.
- **MCP** means the Model Context Protocol interface and curated tool registry through which an authorised application may invoke scoped Platform capabilities.
- **Third-Party Service** means a service, website, platform, model, payment provider, or integration operated by a person other than LineWatt.

## 9. Review, verification, and publication

- **Published** means deliberately made available in the Public Library following an authorised workflow.
- **Review Status** means the recorded stage or outcome of an applicable human or governed review workflow and does not guarantee accuracy, safety, currency, completeness, or suitability.
- **Validation Status** means the recorded result of automated or manual validation and does not guarantee accuracy, safety, currency, completeness, or suitability.

## 10. Billing and commercial terms

- **Enterprise Agreement** means the agreement governing Services supplied to an Enterprise Customer and incorporated Orders.
- **Fees** means amounts due under an Order, checkout, or current plan description.
- **Manufacturer Agreement** means the agreement governing a Manufacturer's controlled participation in the Platform.
- **Payment Provider** means Paddle or another authorised billing provider, acting in the capacity disclosed at checkout and in the Billing and Refund Policy.

## 11. Security and compliance

- **Applicable Law** means laws, regulations, binding regulatory requirements, and court orders applicable to the relevant party, activity, or Services.
- **Confidential Information** means non-public information identified as confidential or reasonably understood to be confidential, excluding information independently developed, lawfully received without duty, or public without breach.
- **Legal Hold** means a documented suspension of ordinary deletion or disposal because information may be required for litigation, investigation, audit, or legal obligation.
- **Material Change** means a change that materially affects legal rights, obligations, risk allocation, processing purposes, access rights, Fees, or acceptance scope.
- **Security Incident** means a confirmed compromise affecting the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of relevant systems, Services, or protected data. A Security Incident may or may not constitute a Personal Data Breach.
