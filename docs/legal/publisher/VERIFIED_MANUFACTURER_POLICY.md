## 1. Purpose and scope

This Policy defines controlled Manufacturer verification and badge use.

## 2. Applicable definitions

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md).

## 3. Meaning and limits

Verified Manufacturer means LineWatt has reviewed specified evidence of organisational identity, domain, representative authority, or another approved factor. It does not certify financial standing, product performance, regulatory conformity, ownership of every submission, or accuracy of every claim and is not an endorsement.

## 4. Evidence and ongoing obligations

Evidence may include official-domain verification, corporate records, signed authority, established public channels, or direct confirmation. Required evidence, review frequency, and storage are `[TO BE CONFIRMED]`. A Manufacturer must report changes in identity, ownership, representatives, domains, or authority and protect its Account.

## 5. Badge and enforcement

Only the Platform may display the official badge unless written brand permission states otherwise. LineWatt may limit, suspend, or remove verification for stale evidence, impersonation, misleading badge use, material breach, unresolved rights or safety concerns, or compromised access, with notice and appeal where appropriate.
