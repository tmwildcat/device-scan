## 1. Purpose and scope

This Agreement supplements the [Terms of Use](../user/TERMS_OF_USE.md) for a Manufacturer admitted through LineWatt's invitation or controlled onboarding workflow.

## 2. Applicable definitions and authority

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md). The accepting representative warrants authority to bind the Manufacturer and must keep authorised representative and company information current. Verification may be required and may be revisited.

## 3. Manufacturer Account and Content

3.1 The Manufacturer may manage authorised profile, product, Source Document, supporting-document, brand, lifecycle, correction, and submission information within enabled entitlements. It cannot publish central records or influence engineering ranking, validation, or approval.

3.2 The Manufacturer retains its rights in Manufacturer Content and warrants it is authentic, current as labelled, lawful, non-confidential unless agreed, malware-free, and licensed sufficiently for the requested use.

## 4. Licence to LineWatt

The Manufacturer grants LineWatt a non-exclusive, worldwide, sublicensable-to-service-providers licence to host, secure, reproduce, transform, extract, normalise, index, review, display, distribute where expressly authorised, link, and create structured Engineering Records and Compiled Datasheets from Manufacturer Content for the Services, attribution, provenance, correction, and enforcement.

The licence ends for future public use after valid withdrawal, except that LineWatt may retain and use necessary copies for legal compliance, security, audit, safety and recall history, dispute evidence, backups, and already lawfully generated outputs; continuing public distribution of a withdrawn Source Document requires an independent right or legal basis.

## 5. Source Documents, marks, and publication

5.1 Hosting or redistribution permission for each Manufacturer PDF must be explicit. Without it, LineWatt may store a controlled processing copy and link users to an authorised Manufacturer source rather than distribute the PDF.

5.2 Marks and brand assets may be used only for accurate attribution, Manufacturer pages, authorised promotion, and Service operation under supplied brand guidance. No ownership transfers.

5.3 LineWatt may create, review, correct, index, approve, reject, publish, supersede, flag, or withdraw structured records. It does not guarantee placement, prominence, search ranking, publication time, or analytics volume.

## 6. Revisions, safety, and corrections

The Manufacturer must promptly identify revisions, obsolete or discontinued products, replacements, recalls, safety notices, regulatory changes, and material errors. LineWatt may preserve historical records with clear status and provenance. Neither verification nor approval endorses every product or claim.

## 7. Confidentiality and data

Each party must protect Confidential Information and use it only for this relationship. Manufacturer representatives' Personal Data follows the Privacy Policy. Unpublished Content remains restricted to authorised review and operations, subject to lawful access and retention.

## 8. Warranties, claims, and risk

The Manufacturer warrants authority and non-infringement to the extent of its licence. Procedures for rights claims follow the [Copyright and Takedown Policy](../governance/COPYRIGHT_AND_TAKEDOWN_POLICY.md). Indemnity, liability cap, insurance, and governing-law terms are `[TO BE CONFIRMED BY COUNSEL]`; they must preserve non-excludable liability.

## 9. Suspension, termination, and survival

LineWatt may suspend submissions or verification for authority failure, material breach, rights claim, safety concern, deception, or security risk. Either party may terminate under `[NOTICE AND CURE TERMS]`. Accrued payment, confidentiality, rights necessary for historical/audit/legal purposes, disclaimers, dispute, and other provisions intended by nature survive.
