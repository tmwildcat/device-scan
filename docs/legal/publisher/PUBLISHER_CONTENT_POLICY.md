## 1. Purpose and scope

This Policy governs Manufacturer and Publisher submissions for LineWatt review.

## 2. Applicable definitions

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md).

## 3. Accepted content

Subject to rights and quality review, LineWatt may accept official datasheets, installation manuals, certificates, warranties, declarations, engineering drawings, and revision, recall, safety, discontinuation, or replacement notices.

## 4. Submission requirements

Content must identify source, product/series, revision, date and language where available; be legible and complete; use accurate titles; distinguish drafts; avoid duplicates; include no unnecessary Personal Data or confidential information; and be free of malware or deceptive links.

## 5. Rejected or restricted content

LineWatt may reject unauthorised, counterfeit, misleading, obsolete-without-label, corrupted, low-quality, unrelated, infringing, malicious, personal, secret, promotional-only, or duplicate Content. Advertising cannot affect validation, ranking, approval, or engineering recommendations.

## 6. Moderation, withdrawal, and appeal

Submissions follow the [Content Review Policy](./CONTENT_REVIEW_POLICY.md). A Publisher may request correction or withdrawal with record URL, authority, reason, and evidence. LineWatt may preserve restricted audit copies and historical metadata. Appeals go to `[PUBLISHER APPEAL ROUTE]` and are reviewed by someone not solely responsible for the initial decision where practicable.
