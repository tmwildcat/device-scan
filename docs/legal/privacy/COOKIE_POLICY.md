## 1. Purpose and scope

This Policy explains cookies and similar browser storage used on the Platform.

## 2. Applicable definitions

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md).

## 3. Categories

LineWatt may use strictly necessary storage for authentication, security, session, load balancing, and consent choices; functional storage for preferences; analytics storage for measurement; and marketing storage only if adopted and lawfully consented to. The authoritative names, providers, purposes, durations, and consent requirements must be recorded in the [Cookie Register](../appendices/COOKIE_REGISTER.md).

## 4. Consent and controls

Non-essential technologies must remain disabled until valid consent where required. Users may change choices through `[COOKIE SETTINGS LINK]` and browser controls; withdrawal must be as easy as consent. Blocking necessary storage may prevent Account features.

## 5. Third parties and changes

Third-party technology may receive device or usage data under its own notice and LineWatt's agreements. New non-essential technology requires privacy review and register/banner updates before release.
