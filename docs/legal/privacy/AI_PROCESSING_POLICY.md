## 1. Purpose and scope

This Policy explains AI-Assisted Processing within document classification, parameter extraction, normalisation, validation support, search, support, and future enabled integrations.

## 2. Applicable definitions

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md).

## 3. Inputs, outputs, and review

Inputs may include Source Documents, Engineering Data, prompts, context, correction feedback, and limited technical metadata. Outputs may include candidate fields, classifications, confidence information, summaries, or tool responses. Deterministic rules and human review may supplement processing. No label or review guarantees correctness.

## 4. Personal Data and providers

Users must avoid unnecessary Personal Data in engineering uploads. Personal Data may be processed where present or required for operation, subject to the Privacy Policy, DPA where applicable, access limits, retention, and approved providers. Third-party model providers, locations, contractual safeguards, and whether inputs/outputs may be used for provider training are `[TO BE CONFIRMED]`; LineWatt makes no “never trained on” claim until evidenced.

## 5. Controls and prohibited uses

Available opt-out, workspace, or provider configuration controls are `[TO BE CONFIRMED]`. Users must not use AI features to evade access controls, expose Private Datasets, generate deceptive publication evidence, or make unverified safety-critical decisions.

## 6. Automated decision-making, correction, and change

Article 22 GDPR concerns certain decisions based solely on automated Processing that produce legal effects or similarly significantly affect a person. LineWatt does not presently consider ordinary DeviceScan extraction and engineering-workflow assistance to fall within that rule because those functions assist Users and are not intended to make such decisions about individuals. This is a provisional legal position, not a guarantee. Each new use must be assessed for automated decision-making, human involvement, profiling, effect, lawful basis, transparency, and safeguards before release. Report errors through `[ENGINEERING CORRECTIONS EMAIL]`; LineWatt may preserve provenance and audit evidence. Material provider or purpose changes require privacy and legal review.
