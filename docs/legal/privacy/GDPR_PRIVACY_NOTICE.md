## 1. Purpose and scope

This Notice supplements the [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md) where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies.

## 2. Identity, roles, and definitions

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md). LineWatt is ordinarily an independent Controller for Accounts, security, billing metadata, and its own analytics. It may be a Processor for Enterprise Customer Personal Data processed solely on documented instructions. Joint controllership applies only under a documented arrangement. Controller, DPO, EU/UK representative, and contact details are `[TO BE CONFIRMED]`.

## 3. Required information

Categories, sources, purposes, legal bases, recipients, transfers, retention criteria, and rights are described in the Privacy Policy and registers. Where data is not obtained directly, likely sources include an organisation, authorised representative, Manufacturer, public professional source, support referral, or Source Document; precise Article 14 disclosures require the verified data map.

## 4. Legitimate interests and consent

Legitimate-interest uses require a recorded balancing assessment, including Platform security, fraud prevention, service improvement, and business-to-business administration. Consent is used only when freely given, specific, informed, recorded, and withdrawable.

## 5. Rights and automated processing

Data Subjects may exercise applicable access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and consent-withdrawal rights and complain to `[SUPERVISORY AUTHORITY]`. LineWatt does not assume every AI-Assisted Processing activity is legally defined automated decision-making; any solely automated decision with legal or similarly significant effects requires separate assessment, safeguards, and disclosure.

## 6. Transfers, retention, and complaints

Transfers outside the relevant area require an adequacy basis or `[APPROVED TRANSFER MECHANISM]`, supplementary assessment where needed, and information on obtaining safeguards. Retention follows documented criteria. Requests go to `[PRIVACY CONTACT EMAIL]` and will be handled within the legally applicable period.
