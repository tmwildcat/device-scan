## 1. Purpose and scope

This Policy explains how LineWatt processes Personal Data when acting as an independent Controller for Platform operation. Enterprise Customer-controlled processing is addressed by the [Data Processing Addendum](./DATA_PROCESSING_ADDENDUM.md).

## 2. Applicable definitions and Controller

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md). The Controller is `[LINEWATT LEGAL ENTITY, ADDRESS AND REGISTRATION DETAILS]`.

## 3. Data collected

LineWatt may process account/profile and authentication data; role and Manufacturer authority evidence; subscription and transaction metadata (not necessarily full card data); uploaded Source Documents and Private Datasets; compilation inputs, jobs and outputs; support and correction communications; search, usage, device and diagnostic data; logs and security events; cookies; and API/MCP request, scope, and audit metadata. Users should not upload unnecessary Personal Data.

## 4. Purposes and legal bases

| Purpose | Typical basis, subject to jurisdictional review |
|---|---|
| provide Accounts, Services, compilation, support and billing | contract; steps before contract |
| secure, debug, prevent abuse and maintain audit trails | legitimate interests; legal obligation |
| comply with law and resolve claims | legal obligation; legitimate interests |
| product analytics and improvement | legitimate interests or consent where required |
| non-essential cookies and marketing | consent where required |

LineWatt must complete a jurisdiction-specific legal-basis assessment before publication.

## 5. Private Datasets and authorised access

Private Datasets are access-controlled and not public by default. Limited personnel or approved providers may access them where necessary for user-requested support or review, security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or reliable operation, using role-based access and audit controls where implemented.

## 6. Recipients, subprocessors, and transfers

Data may be disclosed to hosting, authentication, communications, analytics, support, AI/model, billing, professional-adviser, and public-authority recipients only as necessary and lawful. Confirmed providers and locations belong in the [Subprocessor Register](../appendices/SUBPROCESSORS.md). International transfers require `[TRANSFER MECHANISM]` and applicable safeguards.

## 7. Retention and security

Data is retained by purpose under the [Data Retention Schedule](../appendices/DATA_RETENTION_SCHEDULE.md), subject to deletion requests, backups, fraud prevention, legal hold, and statutory records. LineWatt uses risk-appropriate organisational and technical controls described at a high level in the [Security Policy](../governance/SECURITY_POLICY.md); no system is risk-free.
