## 1. Purpose and scope

This Policy governs lifecycle decisions for Source Documents, Private Datasets, Engineering Records, Personal Data, logs, billing, support, legal, and governance records.

## 2. Applicable definitions and principles

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md). LineWatt retains data only for a documented operational, contractual, safety, evidentiary, or legal purpose; applies the shortest approved period; restricts access; and disposes securely.

## 3. Schedule and ownership

The [Data Retention Schedule](../appendices/DATA_RETENTION_SCHEDULE.md) records event triggers, active and deletion periods, rationale, owner, and disposal method. Placeholders are not authority for indefinite retention.

## 4. Exceptions

Deletion may be delayed for legal hold, active dispute, fraud or abuse investigation, security evidence, statutory records, safety/recall history, or technically isolated backups. Backup data must age out under the approved cycle and not be restored to ordinary use after a valid deletion.

## 5. Private and public engineering data

Private Dataset closure handling must be disclosed before termination and preserve any promised export period. Published Engineering Records may be retained, flagged, superseded, or withdrawn to preserve provenance and safety history; this does not authorise continued public distribution of a withdrawn third-party Source Document.
