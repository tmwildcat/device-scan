## 1. Purpose and scope

This Policy explains the Platform's rights architecture without treating all Engineering Data as owned by one party.

## 2. Applicable definitions

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md).

## 3. Rights model

| Material | Rights position |
|---|---|
| Source Documents, Manufacturer marks/images | Manufacturer or relevant rights holder retains rights; LineWatt needs an express or lawful operational licence |
| User Content and Private Datasets | User retains its rights; LineWatt receives only the operational licence required by the applicable agreement |
| facts, measurements, identifiers, specifications | protection varies; no categorical exclusive LineWatt ownership claim |
| LineWatt Materials | LineWatt or licensors retain software, schema, taxonomy, validation, annotation, compilation, database, search, documentation, brand, and presentation rights as applicable |
| Engineering Records and Canonical Datasets | potentially combine facts, third-party inputs, User rights, and LineWatt selection, arrangement, validation, metadata, or database rights |
| Compiled Datasheets and exports | mixed-rights outputs governed by source rights, format, plan, Order, and export terms |

## 4. Publication and licences

Private processing is not publication. A deliberate Public Library contribution requires rights confirmation and an appropriate licence. Public viewing alone grants no bulk, resale, redistribution, trademark, Source Document, or open-data right.

## 5. Trademarks and feedback

Marks may be used only for accurate identification and authorised attribution; no endorsement is implied. Feedback may be used under the Terms without public attribution of the provider unless permitted.
