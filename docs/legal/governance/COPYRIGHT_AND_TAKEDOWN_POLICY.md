## 1. Purpose and scope

This Policy provides a process for copyright and related-rights complaints involving public Content, hosted Source Documents, exports, or links.

## 2. Applicable definitions

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md).

## 3. Notice requirements

A notice should include claimant identity and contact; authority to act; identification of the protected work; exact Platform location of challenged Content; the right asserted and requested action; supporting evidence; a good-faith belief that use is unauthorised; an accuracy statement; and physical or electronic signature. Send to `[RIGHTS CONTACT EMAIL AND ADDRESS]`.

## 4. Review and interim action

LineWatt may acknowledge, request information, preserve evidence, contact the contributor or Manufacturer, restrict access, replace hosting with an authorised source link, or decline an incomplete or unsupported notice. Safety, privacy, and legal risk inform interim action; restriction is not an admission.

## 5. Response or counter-notice

An affected party may identify the removed location, explain its authority or exception, provide evidence and contact details, consent to `[RELEVANT DISPUTE MECHANISM IF REQUIRED]`, and sign. LineWatt may restore, keep restricted, or seek legal direction after considering both sides and applicable law.

## 6. Repeat infringement and false notices

LineWatt may suspend repeat or serious infringers using a proportionate, documented assessment. Knowingly false or abusive notices may create legal liability and Platform sanctions. Parties should obtain advice before submitting.

## 7. Manufacturer PDFs and related documents

LineWatt does not assume general redistribution rights for third-party Manufacturer PDFs; it may link to an authorised source or host only with permission or another lawful basis. See the [Manufacturer Agreement](../publisher/MANUFACTURER_AGREEMENT.md) and [IP and Licensing Policy](./INTELLECTUAL_PROPERTY_AND_LICENSING_POLICY.md).
