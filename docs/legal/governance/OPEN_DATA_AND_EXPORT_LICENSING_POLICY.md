## 1. Purpose and scope

This Policy governs viewing, export, reuse, caching, attribution, and distribution of Public Library and Private Dataset outputs.

## 2. Applicable definitions

Capitalised terms use [Legal Definitions](../LEGAL_DEFINITIONS.md).

## 3. Public viewing and export

Users may view public Engineering Records through available interfaces. Export formats such as PAN, OND, JSON, CSV, or other supported formats require the relevant plan or Order and may contain mixed rights. Export availability is not a representation that every downstream use is licensed.

## 4. Licence and restrictions

Unless a dataset is separately identified with a specific open licence, Users receive only the limited use right in the controlling agreement. Required attribution is `[ATTRIBUTION FORM]`. Bulk use, commercial redistribution, resale, systematic extraction, database reconstruction, Source Document redistribution, removal of provenance, and use of Manufacturer marks require express permission or an independent legal right.

## 5. Third-party and private rights

Third-party Source Document, Manufacturer, user, privacy, trademark, and format rights continue to apply. Private Dataset exports remain subject to the User's rights and confidentiality duties and do not become public. API/MCP use also follows [API and MCP Terms](../enterprise/API_AND_MCP_TERMS.md).

## 6. Separately licensed datasets

Any open dataset must be listed in `[OPEN DATA REGISTER]` with exact scope, version, licence identifier/text, attribution, excluded fields/assets, provenance, and effective date. At Version 1.0, no dataset is declared open unless that register is completed and approved.
