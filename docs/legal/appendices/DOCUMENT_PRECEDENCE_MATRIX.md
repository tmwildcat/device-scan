## 1. Purpose and scope

This matrix applies only to direct conflicts and remains subject to mandatory law and the Legal Architecture.

## 2. Matrix

| Conflict subject | Controlling document | Then |
|---|---|---|
| negotiated deviation | signed amendment | Order; applicable agreement |
| purchased scope, Fees, term | Order | Enterprise/Subscriber Agreement |
| Customer Personal Data processing | executed DPA | Enterprise Agreement; Privacy Policy for LineWatt-controlled data |
| availability/support credits | incorporated SLA | Order; Enterprise Agreement |
| Enterprise use | Enterprise Agreement | Terms of Use; policies |
| paid individual use | Subscriber Agreement | Terms of Use; policies |
| Manufacturer participation/content licence | Manufacturer Agreement | Terms of Use; publisher policies |
| all Platform access | Terms of Use | applicable incorporated policies |
| engineering reliance | express Order commitment, if any | Engineering Disclaimer; Terms |
| API/MCP use | Order then API and MCP Terms | applicable agreement; AUP |
| privacy notice | applicable law and privacy notice | commercial terms cannot authorise unlawful processing |

## 3. Interpretation

A specific term controls a general one on the same subject. Later text controls only if validly approved, published, notified, and accepted where required.
